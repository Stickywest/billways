4. Group/Chama Loan

Support your group savings goals with our specialized Chama Loans.

  • Eligibility: This loan is granted to an organized and registered group with a minimum of 6 members.
  • Registration fee: Each group member needs to register with a fee of Kshs 500.
  • Shares:
    • Initial purchase: The group must purchase 50 shares (Kshs 100/share).
    • Monthly top-up: Each member contributes a monthly top-up of 15 shares (Kshs 1,500).
  • Loan amount: The group can qualify for a loan amount up to 4 times their total deposits/savings.
  • Collateral: A 25% upfront saving from the group's total is required as security (collateral).
  • Interest rate: The interest rate for this loan is 1% per month.
  • Repayment term: The loan repayment period is set for 3 months.
  • Minimum loan limits:
    • 1st loan: Kshs 30,000.
    • 2nd loan: Kshs 50,000.
    • 3rd loan: Kshs 100,000.
